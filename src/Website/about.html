Our Motivation

Stadium Traffic is dedicated to reducing the carbon footprint due to fans attending sports games in the Philadelphia Area. Recently, there has been a significant push by Philadelphia's sports teams to become more environmentally friendly by launching programs such as the Eagles "Go Green" initiative and Philles "Red Goes Green" initiative.

We believe that traffic emissions, specifically those due to large amounts of time spent idling when entering or leaving the stadium, are a major contributor to the total emissions produced by the stadium and therefore have the potential to be greatly reduced.

The objectives of our project are:
  1. Develop a comprehensive model of transportation to and from the stadium.
  2. Quantify the total emissions due to transportation to and from games.
  3. Optimally route exiting vehicle traffic out of the stadium.
  4. Inform fans of the change in travel time and carbon emissions by using public transport rather than cars.
