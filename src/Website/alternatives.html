Buy Carbon Credits


If you believe it is significantly more convenient to drive to the game, you can offset your carbon emission by buying carbon offsets from TerraPass. "A carbon offset is a certificate representing the reduction of one metric ton (2,205 lbs) of carbon dioxide emissions, the principal cause of climate change." Carpool


Carpooling with people in your neighborhood can greatly reduce your individual carbon footprint, possibly even more than taking SEPTA.
